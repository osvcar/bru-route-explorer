# Meeting notes

## Problem

FAR searches can be slow and do not provide a clear overview of all plausible alternatives, especially when a later regional leg is missing or operates only on specific weekdays.

## Proposed solution

A static route knowledge base that visualises all plausible BRU-to-destination routes before using FAR.

## Operational rule

Origin is always BRU. Routes containing Schengen airports after BRU are excluded.

## Validation

All data is indicative. Final booking and validation remains in FAR.
